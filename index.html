About me

Hey there, thank you for stopping by. I'm Roki, a software developer recently graduated from _nology, a 12-week coding bootcamp. Before the course, I graduated from King's College London with a degree in Global Health and Social Medicine, and I am currently enrolled at Imperial College London to study towards an MPH in Global Public Health.

During my undergraduate studies I started to realise that I needed a more technical understanding of the health care field, so eventually I got more interested in digital health and quantitative data analysis, which led me to coding.

During _nology, I became fascinated with data visualisation, which I find it to be the best way to combine my skills in coding, data analysis, research, and UI/UX design. Therefore, alongside the _nology curriculum, I self-taught a couple of data visualisation libraries (D3.js and Kepler.gl). Those projects are accessible through the links in my CV or my GitHub
